报告题目：Statistics for High-Dimensional Data: Selected Topics

报告人：Professor Peter Bühlmann

报告人单位：Department of Mathematics, ETH Zürich, Switzerland.

报告时间：2014年6月22日—25日上午8：30—11：30

系列报告内容：

  1. High-dimensional linear model: Basic concepts in methodology theory and computation;
  2. High-dimensional nonlinear models: Basic concepts in methodology theory and computation;
  3. High-dimensional inference: p-values and confidence intervals;
  4. High-dimensional causal inference, graphical modeling and structural equation models.

报告地点：中科院数学与系统科学研究院南楼N204会议室

报告人简介：

Peter Bühlmann is Professor of Statistics at Department of Mathematics, ETH Zürich, Switzerland. His research interests are in computational statistics, high-dimensional statistical inference, machine learning and applications in the life sciences. He is a Fellow of the Institute of Mathematical Statistics, an elected Member of the International Statistical Institute. He presented a Medallion Lecture at the JSM 2009 and received a Winton Research Prize in 2013. He has served as editorial member of the Journal of the Royal Statistical Society (Series B), Journal of Machine Learning Research, Biometrical Journal, and he was Co-Editor of the Annals of Statistics from 2010-2012.
